Pufferlib provides compatibility layers for complex environments, optimized parallel simulation, and ultra fast test environments. All our tools are free and open source, but we do offer priority service packages for companies. Demo environment + agent running live 100% client side in your browser. Hold shift to take control!

Pong

A classic reimagined: Play against our reinforcement learned agent or watch AI vs AI matches. Running at 1M+ steps per second directly in your browser.
